Research

I am passionate about the intersection of 3D Vision and Computer Graphics. My current interest lies in exploring neural scene representations to effectively capture the visual, geometric, and physical properties of our surrounding, enabling reconstruction and interaction in virtual environments, especially for dynamic and large-scale scenes. My goal is to develop highly realistic virtual worlds that facilitate immersive experiences in VR/AR, and advance embodied agents' training that can seamlessly generalize to the real world.
